The Problem

We waste food.
And still don’t know what to cook.

Households stare into full fridges every night, then order takeout — while produce spoils and grocery runs stay guesswork.

  • 30–40% of the US food supply is wasted each year — much of it at home
  • $1,500+ average household loses annually to unused groceries
  • “What’s for dinner?” the most exhausting daily decision for busy households

The Solution

Fridge Finder turns one photo into a plan.

Computer vision reads what’s in your fridge. Generative AI turns it into meals you can cook tonight — and a short list of what to buy next.
